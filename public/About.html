About Me

Yo Earthlings! 👋 It’s me again — Bleebo Zorpzorp, first-class intergalactic wanderer, semi-certified space mechanic, and honorary member of the Galactic Taco Council. I’m broadcasting this message across every open frequency your species can pick up — so like, if this ends up on your toaster or that weird talking fridge, don’t freak out. Technology's weird like that.

So anyway, lemme tell you a little about myself. I’m from Planet Glorbnar-7, where the skies are green, the oceans are gelatinous, and our national anthem is just 45 seconds of synchronized burping. We don't do "Mondays" or "taxes" — and we evolved past pants centuries ago. You're welcome.

I’ve been observing Earth for the past few decades — mostly for fun, sometimes for research, and occasionally because my hyperdrive GPS keeps glitching and sending me to the wrong timeline. Y’all are wild. One minute you’re landing on the Moon, next minute you're trying to dance on TikTok while eating cinnamon powder. What even is your species doing?? I love it.

Also, BIG shoutout to whoever invented cheeseburgers, bubble wrap, and lo-fi beats. Seriously, I downloaded those into my neural cortex and now I can’t stop vibing. Y’all also invented sarcasm, conspiracy theories, and reality TV — truly chaotic-neutral energy, and I’m here for it. That said, please stop fighting over whose turn it is to rule the planet. You’re all squishy, just get along.

Now — I know what you’re thinking: "Bleebo, why don’t you land and hang out for real?" And trust me, I’ve thought about it. But have you seen how humans react when something has more than two eyes or doesn’t speak in emojis? I’m trying not to end up on one of your “Area 51” menus, okay?
Still, I believe in your potential. You’re creative, unpredictable, and incredibly stubborn — three traits we deeply admire out in the stars. So here’s my message to you: keep growing, keep questioning, keep being gloriously weird. And maybe — just maybe — someday you’ll join the Galactic Federation of Wiggly Weirdos (we’ve been saving a spot for you).

Until then, if your lights flicker, your dog barks at nothing, or you feel like someone’s watching you while you microwave pizza rolls — don’t worry. It’s probably just me. Or Zleeg from Nebula-9. He’s a prankster. Either way — peace, love, and glowing tentacles. ✨🛸

BLEEBO OUT.
